Packing List Generator
Goal: Create an interactive tool that generates a personalized packing checklist for riders based on discipline(s) and optional preferences.

1. Frontend (User Interface)
A. Step 1 – Select Discipline(s)
Type: Multi-select checkboxes
Options:

Dressage

Show Jumping

Eventing / Cross Country

Combined Training / Arena Eventing

➡️ Logic: If “Eventing” is selected, it should include gear for all three phases (dressage, SJ, XC) and extra detail for riders and horses.

B. Step 2 – Optional Extras
Type: Checkboxes
Options:

I plan to plait

I'm staying overnight

I'm taking a groom/helper

C. Step 3 – Display Custom Checklist
Dynamically generate and display a checklist based on selected options

Each checklist item appears under grouped headings (Rider Gear, Horse Gear, Yard Kit, etc.)

Optional: Let users tick off each item (interactive)

Buttons:

Download PDF

Email checklist to self

Print

2. Backend / Logic Rules
A. Checklist Sections & Items
Rider Gear (always include)
Show jacket

Stock/tie + pin

White shirt

Light-coloured breeches

Gloves (if Dressage or Eventing selected)

Hairnet & show socks

Competition boots

Number bib

If Eventing is selected:

Dressage/show jumping hat

Cross-country hat

Body protector

Eventing watch (optional)

Horse Gear
Dressage (if Dressage or Eventing selected):

Dressage bridle

Dressage saddle

Dressage numnah

Dressage saddle pad

Girth

Show Jumping (if Show Jumping, Combined, or Eventing selected):

Jumping saddle

Jumping bridle

Martingale (if used)

Jumping boots (tendon/fetlock)

Overreach boots

Jumping numnah

Jumping saddle pad

Cross Country (if Eventing selected):

Cross-country bridle and bit

Cross-country saddle (usually same as SJ)

Breastplate

Cross-country boots

Overreach boots

Studs and stud kit (always use studs)

Other Horse Kit (always include)
Headcollar & lead rope

Passport

Rugs (stable, fleece, cooler)

Travel boots

Tail guard

Grooming kit

Hoof pick

Water bucket & sponge

Wash bucket

Feed (if “staying overnight” is selected)

Treats/snacks (optional)

Plaiting kit (if selected)

Yard & Tackroom Kit
Spare tack (girth, reins, stirrup leathers)

Tack cleaning cloths

Mounting/plaiting block

Muck-out tools (if staying overnight)

Helper/Groom Extras (if selected)
Clipboard with schedule

Pen or marker

First aid kit

Folding chair

Phone charger or power bank

Overnight Extras (if selected)
Bedding

Hay & feed

Feed bucket & scoop

Head torch

Spare clothes

Toiletries

Extension lead

Lunge line

3. Export & Output Options
Display:

Organized into collapsible or clearly marked sections

Option to tick off items as packed

Option to hide/show optional items (e.g. studs, plaiting kit)

Export Options:

Download checklist as PDF (grouped by section)

Email checklist (basic form + export to user email)

Print-friendly format
